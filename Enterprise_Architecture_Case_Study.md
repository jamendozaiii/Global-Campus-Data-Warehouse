# Enterprise Data Centralization & Real-Time BI Architecture 
**Project Focus:** The "Hypothesis" & "Industry Magic" Initiatives

## 📌 Project Overview
Before adopting enterprise BI tools, organizations often face the challenge of fragmented data living across various local files. For the "Hypothesis" and "Industry Magic" projects, I engineered a centralized data repository that collated data from multiple sources to report against, serving as a unified source of truth for the company's executive leadership. 

## ⚙️ Decentralized Ingestion & Pipeline Architecture
The primary challenge was tracking functional excellence and workshop effectiveness across a distributed team of Customer Success Managers (CSMs). I designed a scalable architecture utilizing interconnected spreadsheet repositories. 
*   **Data Ingestion:** CSMs utilized localized, dynamic repositories to upload third-party platform data (Zoom workshop metrics, attendee tracking, and product adoption).
*   **Data Transformation:** The architecture dynamically parsed and aggregated this distributed data into a master file, effectively eliminating manual data entry processes.
*   **Real-Time Flow:** This design guaranteed that the overarching system maintained up-to-date data in a timely manner without requiring manual compilation.

## 📊 Executive Stakeholder Delivery
The culmination of this pipeline was a real-time, interactive dashboard platform built directly into the master repository. By deploying dynamic visualizations equipped with custom slicers, I enabled the CEO and the VP of Education to independently monitor key performance metrics. This project demonstrated my ability to build strategic relationships across the business by ensuring appropriate business information and analysis was prepared and accessible for senior stakeholders.
